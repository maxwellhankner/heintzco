About Us

Heintz and Company was established in 1954 and has gone through two name changes since its founding. We are currently staffed by 10 adjusters with an excess of 200 years combined experience. All of our adjusters are licensed and complete the required continuing education to fulfill state requirements. Members of I.A.D.A.

Services

Heintz & Company provides a multi-line claim service to the entire state of Montana, entire state of Wyoming excluding the southeast corner (Cheyenne area). We also service the western North Dakota oil fields and eastern Idaho.


Our services include Automobile, long haul trucking, heavy equipment, agricultural, general liability, bodily injury, physical damage, personal injury, homeowner and commercial property.
